The Mycelium Guild of Durmont

The Weeping Reaches, Durmont The Weeping Reaches, Bavlo, West Ward

General Description

The caves and caverns surrounding the underground metropolis of Durmont produce a staggering amount and variety of mycelium and mycelium-related products, and managing the flow of this massive industry is the Mycelium Guild of Durmont.

The guild has several branch offices on the surface, following paths out from the underworld, in large cities and trade hubs, such as Bavlo. Their presence in these surface settlements is a means of access to wide-spread surface markets for their range of mushroom-based products. Producing goods usable as foodstuffs, medicines, alcoholic beverages, potent drugs, and poisons, there are numerous surface industries which the guild has enriched through their participation.

Bavlo's branch office is more of a logistics office and waystation for the constant stream of goods coming from Durmont. The guild, in coordination with the Durmont Embassy, maintain diplomatic and trade relations with the government and populous of Bavlo.
