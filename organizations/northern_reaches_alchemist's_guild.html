Northern Reaches Alchemist's Guild

The Weeping Reaches, Bavlo, North Ward

General Description

The entirety of the Weeping Reaches region, with its abundance and variety of flora and fauna, is a massive playground for the alchemists of the region. Branch offices of varying size and capacity are spread all over the region, with larger guild complexes in trade hubs offering a range of services to smaller, more isolated offices in distant settlements.
