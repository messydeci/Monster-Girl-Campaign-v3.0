The Sheaves of Truth

The Weeping Reaches, Bavlo, West Ward

General Description

Less of a guild and more of a training academy, the Sheaves of Truth produce competent and versatile accountants and bookkeeps. Governments, guilds, private businesses, and individual merchants all make use of the trained members and services offered by the guard. They operate solely out of Bavlo, but their graduates both come from and go to a wide range of places.
